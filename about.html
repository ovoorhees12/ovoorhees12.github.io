About Stony Brook Ultimate


Who We Are

We are Stony Brook University's official Ultimate Frisbee team. We have a men and women's team, and for several tournaments we combine to compete as a co-ed team. Both teams compete in USA Ultimate's Metro East Division 1 section for a â€‹national ranking. We mostly travel throughout the tri-state area for our weekend tournaments, and attend 8-10 events per school year.

Who Can Join

Any SBU student is welcome to join, both undergrad and graduate students! No prior experience is required! Our team is walk-on, and anyone who consistently shows up to practice will get at least one chance to go to a tournament or other Stony Brook Ultimate event! All you have to do is visit our Contact page, and get in touch with one of our team leaders, or even just show up to practice! Practice times are located on the Schedule page.

About Panic

Panic is our Men's team, within which we have both an A and B team. Both teams compete at local and away tournaments during both the Fall and Spring semesters. Our A team also competes in USA Ultimate sanctioned tournaments.

About Smash

Smash is our Women's team, which has grown from only a few women competing with Panic to it's own team now! Smash also competes in local and away tournaments, including USA Ultimate sanctioned events.

Practices

We practice 3 times a week during both the Fall and Spring, typically either at the South P Lot fields or the Campus Recreation fields. During the beginning of the Spring semester we practice indoors at the Campus Recreation center due to the weather. Practice times are located on the Schedule page.

Tournaments

We usually go to about 4 tournaments per semester, mostly in the tri-state area. During the fall we play at both Men's/Women's and mixed tournaments, and during the Spring we primarily play Men's/Women's. During spring break we head down to Myrtle beach with two mixed teams for the week to compete at High Tide!
